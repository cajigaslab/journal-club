# Exaggerated phase–amplitude coupling in the primary motor cortex in Parkinson disease
**Date:** 2025-11-19
**Presenter:** Pedro Borges
**URL:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3606991/
**Journal:** de Hemptinne et al 2013

## Summary
Examines how basal ganglia disease disrupts cross-frequency phase-amplitude coupling in primary motor cortex. Key mechanism for understanding Parkinson's pathophysiology.

## Key Points
- Cross-frequency coupling between phase and amplitude of different brain rhythms is a key cortical mechanism
- PAC is exaggerated in PD primary motor cortex
